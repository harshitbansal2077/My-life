Moonless Nights

Through the window,
To the moon,
A shine I fear to ever lose.

From the darkness,
To the light,
You lit my world, made it bright.

From strings of heart
To threads of life,
Your presence brings my soul delight.

But moonless nights,
When you're away,
Steal my joy and blur the day.

When you are gone,
When moonlight's drowned,
I want to cry—
I want to drown.
