Threads sewing Hearts

Whats love?
I dont know
What is meant to be loved?
I dont know
What if i tell you i felt it?
You wont believe
But there is a feeling deep down me
Unexplored but someone took it and gave me this.
Meaning of love?
I cannot explain
Just wanting to be with you, my heart exclaimed
You took it and sewed it with yours
Making us one, forever with a cause
You took it what was mine
The trade of love and life
Whats love?
I dont know
But there is a feeling deep down me, yet unexplored
You went to the corner i forgot i had
You made me yours with nothing to be said
Your heart is mine
Mine you made yours
But i got this feeling
Yet unexplored
