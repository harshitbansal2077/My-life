Face of Moon

little heart,
cute smile,
your eyes — just hypnotize.

tainted me,
the pretty you,
your presence — just mesmerize.

the moment I look,
my eyes on you,
those giggles of yours — just beautiful.

my ears,
your voice,
just so soothing — just so bright.

you are my —
forever my wife.

the face of moon,
the moonlit light,
you are — just so perfect, my life.
